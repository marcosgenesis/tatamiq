# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Jiu-jitsu / martial-arts academy students. They open the app on their phone, mostly inside or right after class: to check in (QR), see when their next class is, confirm their belt progress, and keep monthly payments current. Low-frequency, high-emotion moments (a promotion, a paid month) matter more than dense data work. Secondary audience: instructors/admins manage turmas and students from a desktop panel (separate area, already redesigned).

## Product Purpose
Tatamiq runs a martial-arts academy: turmas (class groups), attendance, belt graduation, and monthly fees. The student portal is the athlete-facing slice. Success = a student can check in, know their next class, and track belt + payment status in under 10 seconds, on mobile, without help.

## Brand Personality
Athletic, earned, disciplined. Three words: focused, energetic, proud. The interface should feel like progress on the mats: the belt is the hero, every degree is a milestone. Vibrant, not corporate. Confident orange against clean neutrals.

## Anti-references
- Generic shadcn admin dashboard ported to a phone (the current state: flat gray cards, no hierarchy, no identity).
- Fitness-app neon gradients and glassmorphism.
- Banking-app density. This is not a data tool; it is a few proud moments per week.

## Design Principles
- Belt is the hero. Graduation progress is the emotional core, not a buried tab.
- One-thumb reachable. Bottom nav + center check-in FAB; primary action always in the lower third.
- Status at a glance. Payment, next class, attendance answerable in one look, no drilling.
- Energetic restraint. Orange (#FF4F01) carries action and progress only; neutrals carry everything else.
- Consistent with the admin system's tokens (Inter, orange, 10px radius) but with its own mobile-native, athletic voice.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA. Body text ≥4.5:1 (no muted-gray-on-tint). Touch targets ≥44px. Belt/status never encoded by color alone (always paired with label/icon). Reduced-motion alternative for any progress animation.
